frequently asked questions

What is th space?

th space is an anonymous social media app that connects people based on shared hobbies and interests. Users can engage in meaningful one-to-one conversations and participate in community activities and challenges.

How does the matching process work?

Our algorithms match users based on their interests and hobbies, ensuring you connect with people who share your passions.

Is my identity truly anonymous?

Yes, th space is fully anonymous. Your identity is never revealed, allowing you to interact freely and securely.

What are the houses in th space?

Houses are community groups where users can engage with like-minded individuals. Each house offers a space for discussion, support, and participation in challenges.

What kind of challenges are available?

th space offers various challenges, including 21-day challenges and programs like 75 Hard. These challenges help users build new habits and achieve personal goals.

Can I customize my profile?

Yes, you can personalize your profile by selecting your interests and hobbies. This helps in better matching and interaction with other users.

Are the messages end-to-end encrypted?

While our platform ensures user anonymity and privacy, messages are not end-to-end encrypted.

How do I join a house?

When you sign up, you can choose to join one of the four housesBlue, Green, Red, or Yellow. Each house offers a unique community experience.

What if I face any issues or need help?

Our support team is available to assist you with any issues or questions you might have. You can contact us through the contact section.
